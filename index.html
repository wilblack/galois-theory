Introduction

Galois Thery (pronounced gal-wha) is a subject in mathetics that falls under abstract algebra. The theory was motivated by the question on the existence of solutions to polynomials with rational coefficients. For example $$p(x) = ax^2 + bx + c$$ where a, b, and c or rational numbers.

From high-school algebra we can recall the roots of this polynomial are given by the quadratic equation $$x= \pm \sqrt[2]{\frac{b^2 - 4ac}{2a}}$$
